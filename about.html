hi, my name is Ema!
I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Industrial Design. My work focuses on socially conscious and critical design within urban contexts. My expertise extends to exhibition design, where I've gained valuable experience. I actively engage in workshops that gave me my teamwork skills. I enjoy creating new experiences. I like observing people, their actions, and their thought processes. I draw inspiration from others as well as from within myself and the things I encounter daily. I aim to use design as a catalyst for change and essential communication. I understand that industrial design is the tool through which I can communicate most sincerely and effectively, and I intend to keep using it. I don't want to strictly confine my field of action because I know that for any potential that inspires me, I have the dedication to extract the most from it.
